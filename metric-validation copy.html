Key Case Studies & Regional Evidence

Real-world implementations from Southeast Asian banks and global enterprises

DBS Bank (Singapore)
Harvard Business School documented over SGD 1B in economic returns from AI transformation. McKinsey analysis shows systematic acceleration in people processes through "industrialized" HR data platforms.
BSP Philippines
Deployed NLP-powered chatbot (Proto) to automate HR queries and digitize processes. Streamlined remote onboarding critical in pandemic-disrupted settings with measurable efficiency gains.
Krungsri Thailand
Bank-wide AI fraud systems achieved 35% improvement in detection rates and 18% drop in false positives. Real-time onboarding using digital ID cut processing time by one-third.
FPT Vietnam
PeopleX platform with AI chatbots achieved week-one onboarding completion for frontline roles, representing 50% reduction in onboarding lag for standard jobs across Vietnamese operations.
Hitachi Global
Custom AI digital assistants reduced onboarding by 4 out of 10-15 days (25-40% improvement) while cutting HR team administrative hours by over one-third.
IBM/Amazon/Microsoft
All report structural onboarding and HR workflow time reductions >30% using Workday/AI platforms, with ROI reaching 300% in year one of deployment.
